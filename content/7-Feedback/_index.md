### Overall Evaluation

1. Working Environment
The working environment is quite friendly and relatively comfortable. People in FCAJ are generally willing to support me when I encounter difficulties. The workspace is decent and allows me to focus on completing my tasks.
However, as an intern, I sometimes did not have many opportunities to interact deeply with others.

2. Mentor / Team Admin Support
The mentor provided fairly clear guidance when I faced issues and helped orient me toward solving problems on my own. This helped me improve my thinking and problem-solving skills.
The team admin also supported necessary matters during the internship. However, due to the overall workload, support was sometimes not immediate.

3. Relevance Between Work and Major
The assigned tasks were relatively aligned with my field of study. Some tasks helped me apply what I had learned at university, while also exposing me to new areas.
However, since my practical knowledge is still limited, I needed to spend additional time on self-learning.

4. Learning Opportunities & Skill Development
During the internship, I gained some skills such as researching documentation, working independently, and approaching real-world problems.
However, most of the learning was self-directed, so at times I felt that clearer guidance would help improve learning efficiency.

5. Culture & Team Spirit
The working culture is generally supportive. People work seriously and respect each other. When necessary, there is still coordination among team members.
However, due to the nature of the work, I did not have many chances to be deeply involved in team activities.

Additional Questions

- What did you like most during the internship?
I appreciate having the opportunity to experience a real working environment and gain a better understanding of how AWS services are used in practice.

- What do you think the company should improve for future interns?
It would be helpful to have a clearer roadmap so that interns can better follow and orient themselves during the internship.

- Would you recommend this internship to your friends? Why?
I would still recommend it, especially for those who are good at self-learning. The environment is suitable for developing independence and proactiveness.

Suggestions & Expectations

- Do you have any suggestions to improve the internship experience?
It would be beneficial to add more regular guidance or sharing sessions so that interns can better understand their work.

- Other feedback:
Overall, this was a valuable experience for me, helping me better understand a real working environment, although I still have many things to learn and improve.
