## Worklog Overview

This worklog was carried out over the course of **12 weeks** (approximately 3 months), covering the entire internship period. Each week focused on a specific set of AWS services and DevOps practices, progressing from foundational concepts to a fully deployed, production-style system with CI/CD integration.

The worklog is organized week by week as follows:

Week 1: [Getting familiar with AWS and basic AWS services](http://localhost:55668/1-worklog/1.1-week1/)<br>
Week 2: [Learning core AWS services — IAM, VPC, and RDS](http://localhost:55668/1-worklog/1.2-week2/)<br>
Week 3: [Working with Auto Scaling, Load Balancing, and Monitoring](http://localhost:55668/1-worklog/1.3-week3/)<br>
Week 4: [Exploring CI/CD pipelines and containerization basics](http://localhost:55668/1-worklog/1.4-week4/)<br>
Week 5: [Deploying a full project and preparing a progress report](http://localhost:55668/1-worklog/1.5-week5/)<br>
Week 6: [Deepening IAM permissions and RDS connectivity](http://localhost:55668/1-worklog/1.6-week6/)<br>
Week 7: [Implementing Auto Scaling, CloudWatch, and AWS WAF security](http://localhost:55668/1-worklog/1.7-week7/)<br>
Week 8: [Building CI/CD pipelines with AWS CodePipeline](http://localhost:55668/1-worklog/1.8-week8/)<br>

Throughout these 12 weeks, the work progressed from understanding AWS fundamentals (compute, storage, networking, database) to hands-on practice with security, scaling, monitoring, containerization, and CI/CD automation — culminating in a fully deployed system and a comprehensive final report.
